# Epic 002: Account Overview and Balances

**Epic Description:**  
As a banking customer, I want to view a summary of all my accounts and their balances so that I can easily monitor my financial standing and manage my money effectively.

---

## Goals
- Display all user-linked accounts.
- Show real-time balances.
- Provide account details (masked numbers, types, currencies).
- Ensure secure and efficient data loading.
- Offer navigation to account-specific details.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Epic-level)
- List of all active accounts visible.
- Balances retrieved accurately from backend API.
- Account numbers masked (****1234).
- Responsive dashboard layout.
- Secure data access (only authenticated users).
